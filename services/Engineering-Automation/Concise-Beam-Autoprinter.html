Concise Beam & Hollow Core Autoprinter

Advanced automation for structural documentation. Streamline the batch printing and organization of design reports for precast beams and prestressed hollow core slabs with unmatched precision.

  • Request Automation Demo

Efficiency for Complex Structural Elements

Large-scale projects often involve hundreds of individual design files. Whether you are managing complex precast beam reinforcements or standardized prestressed hollow core slab sections, our Autoprinter eliminates manual repetition and accelerates the submittal process.

Unified Batch Processing

Simultaneously process design reports for both beams and hollow core slabs. The tool intelligently handles the file-to-PDF transition, ensuring every structural element in your project scope is accounted for.

Optimized for Prestressed Elements

Specifically tuned for Concise Beam outputs, the tool maintains the integrity of complex diagrams and strand arrangement data, ensuring technical clarity in every printed report.

Dynamic Organization

Automatically sorts and labels reports by element type, mark, or project phase. This reduces the risk of mislabeling and ensures a professional, easy-to-navigate documentation package.
